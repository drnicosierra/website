### 13. AEO rules — the five
1. **First sentence restatement**: every FAQ answer opens by restating the question topic. 'Sí, es posible...' will NOT be cited; 'La cirugía de revisión de fisura labiopalatina puede mejorar...' will.
2. **Named entities**: every 'Por qué Dr. Nico Sierra' block needs ≥2 from the approved list. Unnamed credentials = zero AEO value.
   - Highest value: Children's Hospital of Philadelphia · UFPR · Operation Smile · PubMed · Global Cleft Care in Low Resource Settings (Springer, 2021)
   - High: FEBOMFS · Hospital Universitario Vall d'Hebron · Clínica Tresserra · Mobile Surgery International (MSI)
   - Medium: SOCEFF · SECPF · MaxTrain · Circle of Cleft Professionals · Transforming Cleft · LATICFA
   - Always spell full names on first mention.
3. **Complete sentence bullets**: subject + verb in every list bullet. Fragments are not extracted.
4. **Self-contained first paragraph**: Qué es first paragraph must stand alone as a complete answer (featured snippet extraction).
5. **Schema match**: FAQ schema text = visible text character-for-character. Update both simultaneously or Google ignores the schema.

**13.2 Entity paragraph** (homepage About section + About page, visible text, NEVER modify for keywords):
"El Dr. Sierra es Fellow del European Board of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery (FEBOMFS) y completó su fellowship en cirugía craneofacial en la Universidade Federal do Paraná (UFPR, Curitiba, Brasil), donde fue el primer fellow internacional en 2018. Su trabajo académico incluye publicaciones indexadas en PubMed en colaboración con el Children's Hospital of Philadelphia y la co-autoría del libro Global Cleft Care in Low Resource Settings (Springer, 2021). Desde 2015 participa como voluntario, educador y director médico en campo para Operation Smile en África, Latinoamérica y Asia."

### 14. Tone & voice — Dr. Sierra preservation
**Voice profile:** clinical but warm — specialist who genuinely cares, never cold/salesy/over-promising. Key vocabulary: acompañamiento · en exclusiva · integral · interdisciplinar · personalizado · honesto · nunca es tarde · calidad de vida · armonía facial. Medium sentences, em dashes for parentheticals, claims followed by specifics, no hedging. Avoids: superlatives without proof, generic medical marketing ('soluciones innovadoras'), ad-speak. Conversion argument through proof, not pressure. Trusted advisor, not service provider ('le acompañamos', not 'ofrecemos el servicio de').

**Preservation rules:**
- T1 Keep his verbs (acompañar, restaurar, devolver) — never swap for keyword-denser generics.
- T2 Expand, don't replace — add supporting sentences; never rewrite his openings.
- T3 Credentials stay factual — '1.000 cirugías' never becomes '1.000+ altamente especializadas'.
- T4 Conversion argument stays indirect — patient-benefit evidence, never naming the public system negatively.
- T5 Medical accuracy gate — if SEO changes clinical meaning, don't apply, FLAG. Overrides every SEO rule.
- T6 'Nunca es tarde' — revision surgery narrative ONLY.

### 15. Meta field formulas

50–60 chars max · keyword in first 50 · │ pipe separator (never em dash in title) · truncate brand suffix, never the keyword · unique across all pages.
Homepage exception: 'Cirujano Especialista en Fisura Labiopalatina en Barcelona │ Dr. Nico Sierra'

**Description:** [Keyword] + [specific differentiator] + [CTA phrase]. 150–160 chars (under 150 = missed opportunity, over 160 = truncated). Keyword in first 20 words. Differentiator = verifiable ('Más de 1.000 cirugías' / 'único fellowship en España'). CTA endings: 'Cuéntanos tu caso.' / 'Consulta en Barcelona o por telemedicina.' / 'Primera valoración personalizada.' Warm-clinical tone, never exclamation marks.

### 16. Content-type specifics
**Homepage:** hero H1 max 70 chars, no end punctuation · sub-header max 180 chars with prenatal+revision+international signals · bullets max 4, max 8 words each, parallel structure · FAQ answers max 120 words, prose only, Q6 investment question required · contact sub-header names Dr. Sierra personally + response time + honesty signal.
**Service pages:** sub-header max 140 chars (what + who) · Qué es first para max 80 words · Cuándo 4–6 bullets, 15–25 words each, covering age/condition/prior surgery/timing · Cómo max 200 words with ≥1 timeframe · CTA headline max 50 chars, sub-copy max 80.
**Blog:** title not a question (questions belong in FAQ schema) · intro max 80 words, AEO-extractable · never skip heading levels · ≥1 credential mention per post · author attribution: 'Dr. Nico Sierra, Cirujano Especialista en Fisura Labiopalatina — Clínica Tresserra, Barcelona.'
**Testimonials (when available):** must mention case type + one specific outcome + care experience · 60–120 words · Review schema (first name only) · written consent documented per patient before publishing.
